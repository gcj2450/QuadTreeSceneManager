Quick Setup:


-To setup your groups for normal LOD.  Simply drag and drop you all you LOD groups into the group lists in the 
inspector.

-You may change the distances between the groups below to your preference.

-To setup groups that instantiate.  Drag your prefabs into the LODinst groups in the inspector.

-You may then control when the objects will instantiate or destroy below.


ENJOY!
